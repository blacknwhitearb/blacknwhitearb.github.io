About me

I cut my teeth and learnt my trade as an arborist in Melbourne, pruning and beautifying the parks and street trees. If you've ever driven around Melbourne, chances are that you have driven past one of my trees.

Now I've come home to Christchurch and have brought my skills with me to use on your garden. It's not all about the trees though, if you have a lawn that needs to be mowed, edges that need sharpening up, or some pesky weeds growing where they're not supposed to, I've got all the right tools for the job.

Call, text or email for a hassle-free quote. I'll listen to you and meet your expectations. Thanks for stopping by, Rick.
